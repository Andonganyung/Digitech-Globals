## 💡 WRITING STYLE GUIDE

**Subtitle tone:** Professional, action-oriented, practical, job-focused, includes "70% hands-on"
**Module tone:** Clear, specific, technical when appropriate, always includes lab
**Lab tone:** Action-oriented, specific deliverable, real-world scenario
**Tools:** Industry-standard, specific versions when relevant
**CTA:** Conversational, benefit-focused, creates urgency

**Keywords to use:**
- Job-ready, industry-focused, hands-on, practical, real-world
- Master, build, develop, implement, configure, deploy
- Enterprise, production, professional, advanced, comprehensive
- Certification-aligned (for cert courses)

**Avoid:**
- Generic phrases, marketing fluff, unrealistic promises
- Overly academic tone
- Passive voice
- Redundant descriptions
