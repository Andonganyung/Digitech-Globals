Professional Slide Design

4 Hours
  • Design principles and best practices

  • White space and visual balance
  • Slide Master customization
  • Lab: Design custom branded template

Module 3: Animations & Transitions

3 Hours
  • Slide transitions and effects

  • Motion paths and custom animations
  • Animation timing and sequencing
  • Morph transition for seamless movement
  • Lab: Create animated product demo

Module 4: Data Visualization

4 Hours
  • Creating and formatting charts
  • SmartArt graphics for concepts
  • Tables and data presentation
  • Infographic design techniques
  • Data-driven slide layouts
  • Lab: Build data dashboard slides

Module 5: Images & Graphics

3 Hours
  • Inserting and formatting images
  • Image cropping and effects
  • Icons and shapes usage

  • Lab: Create visual-heavy presentation

3 Hours
  • Embedding videos and audio
  • Video trimming and formatting
  • Hyperlinks and action buttons

2 Hours
  • Comments and review features

3 Hours
  • Presenter View mastery
