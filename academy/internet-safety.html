Internet Safety & Online Security

Stay safe online - protect yourself from scams, phishing & hackers.

8 Hours
22 Lessons
4.9 Rating
7,500+ Enrolled

What You'll Learn

Stay safe online! Learn to protect yourself from scams, phishing attacks, identity theft, and cyber threats. Essential knowledge for anyone using the internet.

Key Skills You'll Master

Recognizing Scams & Phishing

Identify fake emails, suspicious links, phishing websites, and common online scams before they steal your information.

Strong Password Management

Create unbreakable passwords, use password managers, enable two-factor authentication, and protect your accounts.

Safe Browsing Practices

Verify website security (HTTPS), avoid malicious downloads, use ad blockers, and browse privately when needed.

Social Media Privacy

Control privacy settings on Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn. Understand what to share and what to keep private.

Protecting Personal Information

Safeguard social security numbers, credit cards, banking details, and prevent identity theft online.

Malware & Virus Prevention

Recognize malware, use antivirus software, avoid ransomware, and recover from security incidents safely.

Hands-On Labs & Projects

Lab 1: Phishing Detection

Analyze real phishing email examples, identify red flags, verify sender authenticity, and practice safe email habits.

Lab 2: Password Manager Setup

Install password manager, create master password, migrate existing passwords, enable two-factor authentication.

Lab 3: Browser Security

Configure browser privacy settings, install security extensions, manage cookies, test HTTPS connections.

Lab 4: Social Media Lockdown

Audit Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn privacy settings, limit visibility, control who sees posts, manage apps.

Lab 5: Safe Online Shopping

Verify website legitimacy, use secure payment methods, recognize fake shopping sites, protect credit cards.

Lab 6: Credit Monitoring

Sign up for free credit monitoring, understand credit reports, set up fraud alerts, freeze credit if needed.

Lab 7: Antivirus Protection

Install antivirus software, run full system scan, schedule automatic scans, update virus definitions.

Project: Complete Security Audit

Perform full security review of all accounts, devices, and practices. Implement all learned protections.
