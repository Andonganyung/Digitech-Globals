Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time from your dashboard. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.

Do I keep my certificates after canceling?

Absolutely! All certificates you earn are yours forever. You can download and share them even after your subscription ends.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for Enterprise plans.

Can I switch between plans?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing date, and we'll prorate any differences.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes! Students with a valid .edu email address get 20% off all plans. Contact us with your student ID to get your discount code.

How do live Q&A sessions work?

Pro members get access to live sessions with instructors where you can ask questions, get career advice, and learn advanced topics. Sessions are recorded if you can't attend live.
