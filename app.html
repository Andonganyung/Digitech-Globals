Install on iPhone / iPad

Add the app to your home screen for quick access - works just like a native app!

  1. 1

    Open Safari Browser

    Make sure you're using Safari (not Chrome or other browsers). This feature only works in Safari on iOS.

  2. 2

    Go to the App

    Visit andonganyung.github.io/FedTeck-app or tap the "Launch App" button above.

  3. 3

    Tap the Share Button

    Tap the Share icon (square with arrow pointing up) at the bottom of Safari.

  4. 4

    Select "Add to Home Screen"

    Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen". You may need to scroll right to find it.

  5. 5

    Name the App & Tap "Add"

    Keep the name as "DigiTech" or customize it, then tap "Add" in the top right corner.

Tip: The app icon will appear on your home screen. Tap it anytime to access your client portal!

Install on Android

Add the app to your home screen for a native app experience!

  1. 1

    Open Chrome Browser

    Use Google Chrome for the best experience. Other browsers may also work.

  2. 2

    Go to the App

    Visit andonganyung.github.io/FedTeck-app or tap the "Launch App" button above.

  3. 3

    Tap the Menu (3 dots)

    Tap the three dots in the top-right corner of Chrome to open the menu.

  4. 4

    Select "Add to Home Screen"

    Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app" from the menu.

  5. 5

    Confirm Installation

    Tap "Add" or "Install" to confirm. The app will be added to your home screen.

Tip: Some Android phones may show an "Install" banner at the bottom - just tap it for quick installation!

Install on Windows PC

Install the app on your Windows computer for quick access from your desktop or taskbar!

  1. 1

    Open Chrome or Edge Browser

    Use Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge for installation support.

  2. 2

    Go to the App

    Visit andonganyung.github.io/FedTeck-app or click the "Launch App" button above.

  3. 3

    Look for the Install Icon

    In Chrome: Look for the install icon (computer with down arrow) in the address bar, or click the three dots menu.
    In Edge: Look for the "App available" icon in the address bar.

  4. 4

    Click "Install"

    Click "Install" when prompted. The app will be installed on your computer.

  5. 5

    Access from Start Menu or Desktop

    The app will open in its own window. You can find it in your Start Menu or pin it to your taskbar.

Tip: Right-click the app in your taskbar and select "Pin to taskbar" for quick access!

Install on Mac

Install the app on your Mac for quick access from your dock or Launchpad!

  1. 1

    Open Chrome or Edge Browser

    Use Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge. Safari doesn't support PWA installation on Mac.

  2. 2

    Go to the App

    Visit andonganyung.github.io/FedTeck-app or click the "Launch App" button above.

  3. 3

    Look for the Install Option

    In Chrome: Click the three dots menu ‚Üí "Save and Share" ‚Üí "Install Page as App" or look for install icon in address bar.
    In Edge: Click the three dots menu ‚Üí "Apps" ‚Üí "Install this site as an app".

  4. 4

    Click "Install"

    Confirm by clicking "Install". The app will be added to your Applications folder.

  5. 5

    Access from Launchpad or Dock

    Find the app in Launchpad or your Applications folder. Drag it to your Dock for quick access.

Tip: After opening the app, right-click its icon in the Dock and select "Options" ‚Üí "Keep in Dock"!
