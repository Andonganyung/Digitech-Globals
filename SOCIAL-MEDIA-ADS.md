## Targeting Recommendations

### Primary Audiences
1. **Career Changers** (25-45 years old)
   - Interests: Career development, online learning, job searching
   - Behaviors: Recently viewed job listings, education content

2. **Students/Recent Grads** (18-28 years old)
   - Interests: Technology, gaming, computers
   - Education: Some college, no degree

3. **IT Professionals** (25-50 years old)
   - Interests: Microsoft, AWS, networking, cybersecurity
   - Behaviors: Engaged with tech content, certification seekers

### Geographic Focus
- Primary: United States (Maryland, Virginia, DC area)
- Secondary: Global English-speaking markets
- Tertiary: Africa (Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, South Africa)
